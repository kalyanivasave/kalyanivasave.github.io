AutoMatch.AI: Intelligent Job Matching Workflow

An n8n-powered AI workflow that automatically parses LinkedIn job posts, scores resume-to-job relevancy using OpenAI, and sends instant alerts for high-fit opportunities, eliminating the need for manual job searching.

Job searching is time-consuming and inefficient. Professionals spend hours manually browsing job boards, tailoring resumes for mismatched roles, and missing opportunities that truly align with their background.
