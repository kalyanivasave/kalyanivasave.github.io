Featured Case Study
Healthcare • EHR Usability • Product Innovation

SmartDoc: Pre-Visit Summary Tool for Doctors

Designed a floating summary panel that consolidates critical patient data, including visit reason, vitals, chronic conditions, medications, and red flags, to reduce physician cognitive load and EHR tab-switching fatigue.

Problem

70% of outpatient doctors struggle to find relevant patient information due to fragmented EHR systems, spending over 50% of time on EHR interaction vs. only 27% with patients.

Solution & Hypothesis

Rule-based pre-visit summary tool to reduce prep time by 25%, improve care quality, and lower EHR fatigue through scan-optimized layout and smart data grouping.

Research & Validation

Evidence from JAMA, Stanford Medicine, NIH studies; analyzed live EHR interfaces (PrognoCIS) to identify friction points.

Featured Case Study
E-commerce • Social Commerce • UX Design

Amazon Social: Reimagining Product Discovery Within Amazon

Embedded community-driven product discovery directly into Amazon's ecosystem through a dedicated Social Feed, addressing the gap where shoppers leave Amazon to validate purchases on external platforms.

Problem

Shoppers currently leave Amazon to validate purchases on YouTube, Reddit, or Instagram, creating discovery gaps and friction. Amazon lacks community-driven social proof within its ecosystem.

Solution & Features

Dedicated Social Feed inside Amazon app with posts from trusted influencers and Amazon Vine reviewers, engagement features (like, comment, repost), niche communities, and early-purchase offers.

Design Process & Validation

Sprint-based UX process: user research on external validation patterns, competitive analysis vs TikTok/Twitter, Crazy 8s sketching, high-fidelity prototyping, and testing with 50+ participants.

Featured Case Study
EdTech • Data Science • Machine Learning

Student Dropout Prediction: Early Intervention ML Model

Developed predictive models using 10 years of student data to identify at-risk students early, enabling universities to design effective retention strategies and reduce dropout rates.

Problem

Universities struggle to identify at-risk students early, leading to high dropout rates and reduced academic success. Traditional approaches lack predictive insights for proactive intervention.

Solution & Approach

Machine learning models using demographic, academic, and financial features to predict student outcomes (dropout, enrolled, graduate) with Decision Tree and Random Forest algorithms.

Data & Methodology

SATDAP-supported dataset spanning 10 years (2008/09–2018/19) of undergraduate data. Features include financial status, academic grades, application mode, and previous qualifications.

AutoMatch.AI: Intelligent Job Matching Workflow

Built an n8n-powered AI workflow that automatically scrapes LinkedIn jobs, scores resume-to-job relevancy using OpenAI, and sends real-time Telegram alerts for high-fit opportunities, eliminating manual job searching.
