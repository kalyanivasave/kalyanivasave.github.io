About Me

Hi, I'm Kalyani Wasave, a product professional with over 6.5+ years of experience across enterprise systems, digital platforms, and education technology.

My background spans product ownership, business systems analysis, PLM/ERP implementation, and data analytics. I've worked across industries including manufacturing, e-commerce, and most recently K-12 edtech platform, where I led 0→1 product development for a B2B EdTech platform. That experience deepened my interest in building tools that are not just functional but genuinely useful, products that reduce friction, support real workflows, and create meaningful impact for the people using them.

What ties my work together is an approach rooted in understanding the problem before defining the solution. Whether I was mapping legacy ERP systems at MillerKnoll, designing data governance frameworks, or translating educator and student needs into product requirements, the common thread has always been turning complexity into clarity. Structured requirements, well-defined roadmaps, and delivery that aligns with real business and user needs.

I'm naturally drawn to work that sits at the intersection of people, technology, and everyday problems. I care about strong user understanding, thoughtful product decisions, and building things with intention, whether that means defining workflows, improving usability, or shaping early-stage product direction from ambiguity into something concrete and actionable.

At the core, I'm someone who likes learning, observing, and building with purpose. I care about thoughtful product decisions, strong user understanding, and creating things that are genuinely helpful. If my work resonates with you, I'd love to connect.

Healthcare • EHR Usability • Product Innovation

SmartDoc: Pre-Visit Summary Tool for Doctors

Designed a floating summary panel that consolidates critical patient data, including visit reason, vitals, chronic conditions, medications, and red flags, to reduce physician cognitive load and EHR tab-switching fatigue.

Problem

70% of outpatient doctors struggle to find relevant patient information due to fragmented EHR systems, spending over 50% of time on EHR interaction vs. only 27% with patients.

Solution & Hypothesis

Rule-based pre-visit summary tool to reduce prep time by 25%, improve care quality, and lower EHR fatigue through scan-optimized layout and smart data grouping.

Research & Validation

Evidence from JAMA, Stanford Medicine, NIH studies; analyzed live EHR interfaces (PrognoCIS) to identify friction points.

AI/Voice • Healthcare • Real-time Streaming

VoiceCare AI: Intelligent Dental Receptionist Assistant

Built a voice-native AI agent using Google Gemini Live API to autonomously manage appointment scheduling, patient intake, and insurance verification in real-time (<300ms latency), reducing booking time from 4 minutes to under 90 seconds.

Problem

Dental practices lose ~25% of revenue due to missed calls and friction in manual scheduling. Traditional IVR systems are rigid and frustrating, forcing patients through decision trees instead of natural conversation.

Solution & Architecture

Developed an always-on AI receptionist using Google Gemini Multimodal Live API for emotionally intelligent, low-latency voice conversations. The system uses streaming architecture to handle audio input/output and state management simultaneously, transforming unstructured voice data into structured JSON in real-time.

Key Capabilities & Impact

Real-time Voice Streaming: WebSocket bidirectional audio with natural interruption capability. Structured Data Extraction: Function calling to parse names, dates, medical info into validated JSON. Success Metric: Reduced "Time to Booking" from 4 minutes (manual) to <90 seconds (AI). Multilingual Support: English, Spanish, Hindi, Mandarin, and Arabic.
