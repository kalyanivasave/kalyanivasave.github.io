About Me

I’m a product and business strategist who loves turning complex ideas into clear, human-centered solutions. My work lives at the intersection of data, design, and storytelling — where analytics guide the “why,” creativity shapes the “how,” and empathy drives the “what.”

With over 7.5 years of experience across B2B SaaS, analytics, and telecommunications, I’ve helped shape products and go-to-market strategies that improved engagement, conversion, and operational efficiency. Whether leading launches at MillerKnoll, driving insights at Datamatics, or expanding markets at Reliance Jio, I’ve learned to connect business outcomes with real user value.

I also built my own e-commerce venture, handling everything from brand storytelling to customer acquisition — an experience that taught me ownership, adaptability, and creative problem-solving. Beyond product work, I explore AI-driven storytelling, video creation, and digital content, using creativity to turn ideas into narratives that inspire and engage.

Currently pursuing my Master’s in Information Systems at Northeastern University, I’m expanding my expertise in Generative AI, product innovation, and data strategy.

Previously, I earned a Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics and Telecommunications from MIT Pune (University of Pune).
