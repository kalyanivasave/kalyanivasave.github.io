The Problem

Universities struggle to identify at-risk students early, leading to high dropout rates. Traditional approaches are reactive rather than predictive.

Late identification of struggling students
Limited predictive insights
Inefficient resource allocation

The Solution

Machine learning model analyzing demographic, academic, and financial data to predict three outcomes: dropout, stay enrolled, or graduate.

Key Features

  • Early risk identification
  • Multi-class prediction
  • Stable Random Forest model
  • 10-year historical analysis
