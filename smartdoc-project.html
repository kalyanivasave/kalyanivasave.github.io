SmartDoc: Pre-Visit Summary Tool

Floating summary panel that consolidates critical patient data to reduce physician cognitive load and EHR tab-switching fatigue.

The Problem

70% of outpatient doctors struggle to find relevant patient information due to fragmented EHR systems, spending over 50% of time on EHR vs. only 27% with patients.

Information fragmentation across tabs
High cognitive load during appointments
Reduced patient face time

The Solution

Floating summary panel that intelligently consolidates critical patient data, including visit reason, vitals, chronic conditions, medications, and red flags, in a single, scannable view.

Key Features

  • Rule-based information priority
  • Scan-optimized layout design
  • Smart data grouping
  • Red flag highlighting system
