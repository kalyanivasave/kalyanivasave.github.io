Amazon Social: Reimagining Product Discovery

Embedding community-driven product discovery directly into Amazon's ecosystem without disrupting its core shopping experience.

The Problem

Shoppers leave Amazon to validate purchases on YouTube, Reddit, and Instagram, creating discovery gaps and potential for competitive influence.

External validation dependencies
Trust gaps within Amazon
Discovery friction for new products

The Solution

Dedicated Social Feed inside Amazon app with posts from trusted influencers and Amazon Vine reviewers, enabling community-driven discovery without disrupting core commerce.

Key Features

  • Social feed with trusted content
  • Engagement & community features
  • Niche product communities
  • Seamless shopping integration

Core Features

• Social Feed

Personalized posts from trusted influencers and Amazon Vine reviewers with authentic product recommendations.

• Engagement

Like, comment, repost, and quote posts for authentic social proof and community interaction.

• Follow System

Follow trusted voices for curated recommendations and build personalized discovery experiences.

• Post Creation

Share purchases with photos, videos, tags, and direct product links for authentic reviews.

• Communities

Join niche groups like Home Bakers, Travel Essentials for specialized product discovery.

• Metrics

View engagement insights for social validation and community trust building.

User Feedback & Iterations

• Users loved: Familiar social feel inside Amazon's UI, trusted reviewer integration
• Challenge solved: Navigation duplication, simplified interface in later iterations
• Key improvement: Clearer separation between shopping and social actions

Competitive Advantage

Unlike TikTok or Twitter's social commerce, Amazon Social leverages existing trust and shopping infrastructure while adding community discovery—keeping users within the ecosystem rather than losing them to external platforms.
